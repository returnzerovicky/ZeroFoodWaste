About NoFoodWaste AI

Our Mission: To fight hunger and food waste by connecting surplus meals to those in need through technology and community effort.

Why It Matters: Millions go hungry while edible food is thrown away. We aim to bridge that gap.

Future Plans:

  • AI-based food matching (auto-pair donors and NGOs)
  • Smart routing for pickups
  • Analytics for food waste tracking
